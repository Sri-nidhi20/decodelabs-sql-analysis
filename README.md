# SQL Data Analysis Project - Decodelabs Internship

## Project Overview

This project was completed as part of the DecodeLabs Data Analytics Internship Program.

The objective of this project was to analyze an Ecommerce Sales Dataset using SQL and generate meaningful business insights related to revenue, customer behavior, product performance, payment methods, and marketing channels.

The analysis focuses on transforming raw business data into actionable recommendations that can support data-driven decision making.

---

## Business Objectives

- Analyze overall business revenue
- Identify top-performing products
- Understand customer purchasing behavior
- Evaluate payment method performance
- Identify high-value customers
- Analyze order status distribution
- Evaluate referral source effectiveness
- Generate business recommendations

---

## Dataset Information

The dataset contains ecommerce transaction records including:

- Order ID
- Customer ID
- Product Information
- Quantity
- Unit Price
- Total Price
- Payment Method
- Order Status
- Referral Source
- Transaction Date

### Dataset Size

- Total Records: 1200
- Total Features: 14

## SQL Business Questions Solved

### Query 1: Total Revenue Analysis
Calculated overall business revenue.

### Query 2: Revenue by Product
Identified products generating the highest revenue.

### Query 3: Average Order Value
Calculated average customer spending per order.

### Query 4: Orders by Payment Method
Analyzed customer payment preferences.

### Query 5: Order Status Analysis
Evaluated order distribution across statuses.

### Query 6: Top Customers by Revenue
Identified highest-value customers.

### Query 7: Revenue by Payment Method
Compared revenue contribution by payment channel.

### Query 8: Products by Quantity Sold
Analyzed product demand and sales volume.

### Query 9: Revenue by Order Status
Evaluated revenue across different order lifecycle stages.

### Query 10: Referral Source Performance
Identified the most effective customer acquisition channels.

---

## Key Findings

- Total business revenue exceeded ₹1.26 Million.
- Chair generated the highest revenue and sales volume.
- Average order value was ₹1053.97.
- Credit Card transactions generated the highest revenue.
- High-value customers contributed significantly to total revenue.
- Instagram was the strongest referral source.
- Product demand varied across categories.
- Multiple payment methods were actively used by customers.
- Data-driven insights can improve business performance and profitability.

---

## Business Recommendations

### Product Strategy
Maintain adequate inventory levels for high-demand products and improve visibility of lower-performing products.

### Customer Retention
Develop loyalty programs and personalized engagement strategies for high-value customers.

### Payment Optimization
Improve the checkout experience for high-performing payment methods and encourage digital payments.

### Marketing Strategy
Allocate additional marketing resources to high-performing acquisition channels such as Instagram and Email marketing.
